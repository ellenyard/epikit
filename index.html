Features

Import CSV & Excel

Bring in data with worksheet selection and smart date-format handling, or paste straight from a spreadsheet.

Review & clean

Data-quality checks, line-list editing, derived variables, and a full edit history you can audit and export.

Epidemic curves

Build epi curves with stratification, annotations, and incubation-period overlays in a few clicks.

Maps

Spot maps, area (choropleth) maps, and freehand sketch maps for transmission scenarios and field settings.

Statistics & 2×2

Descriptive stats, frequency tables, cross-tabulations, and 2×2 analysis with the measures epidemiologists expect.

Chart gallery & exports

A publication-oriented gallery (bar, dot, forest, slope, waffle and more) with clean image exports.

Training datasets & tutorials

Synthetic outbreak datasets and embedded, step-by-step tutorials to learn each tool as you go.

Portable projects

Export a whole project to a single file and import it on another device. A simple, cloud-free backup.

Locale & accessibility

Configurable number and date formats plus accessibility controls for diverse teams and settings.

Your data stay in your browser

Imported datasets are processed locally and saved only in your browser's storage. LineList does not upload your data to an application server or offer cloud dataset storage. Map tiles and other externally hosted resources generate normal network requests.

Do not import protected health information or direct identifiers. De-identify datasets and follow your organization's policies before use.
